Connect landing pages you create in Infusionsoft to your WordPress site so the leads automatically get added as contacts in your Infusionsoft account.

== Description ==

With Infusionsoft’s landing page plugin for WordPress, you can connect the landing pages you create and publish to campaigns in your Infusionsoft account to your Wordpress website. That way, leads generated from your website will automatically get added as contacts in your Infusionsoft account.

Follow these steps to publish Infusionsoft landing pages on your WordPress website:

* Build and publish your landing page in Infusionsoft
* Install this plugin
* Copy your landing page website address (URL) to the WordPress plugin
* Assign a new page name (slug) that you’d like the landing page to appear on

Now, you’ll be able to launch your landing page on your own domain without needing to know how to code!
